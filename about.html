Our Story

Ireland – December 22, 2020 – A local man’s dreams come to fruition after 25 years. Local Chef and pizza fanatic Philip Edwards opened Arty Zano's. A native of Dublin, he spent years honing his love of the perfect pizza in New York, San Francisco, where he met his wife Gabriella, Sydney and Thailand. He retuned home in 2019 with a simple vision: to share his passion for food and his love of art. Since he returned home he has worked tirelessly to create a magical pizzeria full of warmth and flavour.

“This has been my dream since I worked in a pizza place as a youth, I am absolutely ecstatic”, said Edwards. He added “The people of Ireland have always been so warm & friendly, we think of it like working in paradise. We are really excited about sharing some amazing flavours in our authentic Italian food.”

Edwards & his wife will personally operate the gourmet pizzeria. They plan to employ at least twenty other staff. The pizzeria offers carryout & free delivery with an eye on customer service.
