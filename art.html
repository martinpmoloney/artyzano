ANGELICA DIVINE

ANGELICA DIVINE

Born in rural Provence, France, Angelica Divine moved with her parents to London when she was ten. The years she spent growing up in the urban centre of a busy city created a yearning for the simpler life she knew as young child. A keen artist she used her craft as expression of her inner love for tradition.

Whilst studying at the Royal College of Art in London, she perfected her skill with oil paint and mirroring the techniques of the Renaissance masters. It was here that she met her husband Gabriel. When they married they moved to Ireland and set up her studio near Tara, in Co. Meath. The rural countryside that surrounds her now inspires her art and her retrospective and classical style.

We are very excited to have Angelica display at our Gallery. Check the calendar for our next Meet the Artist evening and upcoming auction.
